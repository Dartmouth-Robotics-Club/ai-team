# About

The Dartmouth Student Robotics Team (DSRT) is a student-run team of current [Dartmouth][dartmouth] students
who are passionate about the engineering and design of robots.
We have dedicated subteams that focus on various aspects:

- Competitive Robotics
- Robotics Product Design
  - Mechanical Design
  - Electrical Engineering
  - <span className="text-red-500">AI Components in Robotics.</span>

This site a project log and learning center for the AI team under product design.
If you are interested in something else, try the [main site][dsrt].
